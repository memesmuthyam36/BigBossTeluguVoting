Frequently Asked Questions

How does the voting system work?

Our voting system allows you to vote for your favorite contestants once per day. Votes are tracked using cookies and IP addresses to ensure fair voting. You can vote for any contestant by clicking on their card.

Can I submit my own memes?

Yes! We love user-generated content. You can submit your memes through our contact form or tag us on social media. The best memes will be featured on our blog with proper credit.

How often is the content updated?

We update our content daily during the Bigg Boss season. New memes, voting updates, and blog posts are published regularly to keep you engaged with the latest happenings.

Is the website mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! Our website is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. You can vote, read articles, and share content seamlessly on any device.

How can I collaborate with you?

We're always open to collaborations! Whether you're a content creator, influencer, or brand, reach out to us through our contact form with your collaboration proposal.

Do you have a mobile app?

Currently, we don't have a mobile app, but our website is fully optimized for mobile browsers. We're working on a mobile app that will be available soon!
