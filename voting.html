Bigg Boss show came to India from another country. At that time, as smartphones, internet, and social media were not available, voting was done by the missed call method. This missed call method is the oldest in the history of Bigg Boss and continues till date. Each contestant is allotted a number and you can give 1 missed call per day to that number, each missed call counts as one vote - TV streaming services.
