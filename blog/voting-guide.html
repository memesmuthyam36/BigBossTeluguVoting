The Bigg Boss Telugu voting system operates through multiple platforms, giving viewers flexibility in how they cast their votes. Here's what you need to know:

How to Vote for Bigg Boss Telugu Online - Step by Step

Method 1: Official Voting via Disney+ Hotstar

  1. Download Disney+ Hotstar App: Install the app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store
  2. Login or Sign Up: Create an account or log in with your existing credentials
  3. Find Bigg Boss Telugu: Search for "Bigg Boss Telugu 9" or navigate to the show page
  4. Access Voting Section: Click on the "Vote" button displayed on the show page
  5. Select Contestant: Choose your favorite nominated contestant from the list
  6. Submit Vote: Click "Submit" to cast your vote (you can vote up to 10 times per day)

Bigg Boss Telugu Voting Rules 2025

Understanding the Bigg Boss Telugu voting rules is crucial to ensure your votes count:

  • You can cast up to 10 votes per day on the official Hotstar app
  • Voting is only available for nominated contestants
  • Voting typically starts Monday morning and closes Friday evening
  • You must have a valid Disney+ Hotstar account to vote officially
  • All votes must be cast before the deadline - late votes won't count
  • One person can vote for multiple contestants (distribute your 10 votes)
  • Voting is completely free on both official and fan platforms

Common Bigg Boss Telugu Voting Questions

Q: Is Bigg Boss Telugu voting free?

A: Yes! Voting is completely free on both Disney+ Hotstar (official) and community platforms like Memes Muthyam. You don't need to pay anything to cast your vote.

Q: Can I vote for multiple contestants?

A: Yes! You can distribute your 10 daily votes across multiple contestants or give all 10 votes to one contestant.

Q: When does Bigg Boss Telugu voting close?

A: Voting typically closes on Friday evening around 11:59 PM before the weekend elimination episode. Check the official app for exact timings.

Q: Can I see live voting results?

A: Official results are not publicly displayed by Hotstar. However, our community voting page shows live poll results so you can gauge fan sentiment.
